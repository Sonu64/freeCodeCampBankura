About FreeCodeCamp Bankura

FreeCodeCamp Bankura is freeCodeCamp.org's Local Study Group in the city of Bankura (West Bengal, India). At FreeCodeCamp, millions learn to code and create. Local Study Groups, though unofficial are completely Non-Profit. Anyone who wants to learn to code can join us. Joining process is extremely simple. Go to the Join Us page for more details.
